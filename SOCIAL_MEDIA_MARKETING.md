## Engagement Tips

1. **Post consistently** — aim for at least 4-5 times per week
2. **Best posting times in Botswana:** 12-1pm (lunch) and 6-8pm (evening)
3. **Always respond to comments within 2 hours**
4. **Use Instagram Reels** — short videos of treatments get high engagement
5. **Collaborate with local influencers** — offer free services in exchange for posts
6. **Run giveaways** — "Tag a friend who needs a spa day" for engagement
7. **Share user-generated content** — repost client photos (with permission)
8. **Use location tags** — always tag Phakalane and Gaborone
9. **Create a branded hashtag** — #EPWellnessExperience
10. **Partner with wedding vendors** — photographers, venues, dress shops
